Enyaq Coupé: Intelligent Technology Canton Sound System The Škoda Enyaq Coupé can be equipped with an audio system featuring 12 speakers and a digital equalizer. The complete sound system has been coordinated with the interior of the Enyaq Coupé in collaboration with the German company Canton, which specializes in the development and production of speakers and audio systems. You can choose from several predefined audio profiles (Canton, Speech, Rock, and Philharmonic). The sound can be adjusted to the number of passengers in the vehicle (Modes All, Front, and Driver), ensuring the perfect sound of your favorite music at all times.
