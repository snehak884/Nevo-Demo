Škoda Octavia Combi – Connectivity Škoda Connect With Škoda Connect¹, you have access to two types of online services. While Infotainment Online offers you information services in real-time, Care Connect focuses on service and safety features. With Škoda Connect, you can remotely control selected vehicle functions through the MyŠkoda App and benefit from practical assistance services. 1. Škoda Connect includes Infotainment Online and Care Connect. Services depend on the model, may involve charges, and their availability depends on the country and mobile reception. Registration with Škoda Auto a.s. is required, except for e Call. Infotainment Online can only be used with a Škoda navigation system. Care Connect (Remote Access) is controlled via the MyŠkoda App, which requires a smartphone (i OS or Android) with internet access. For more information about Škoda Connect, please contact your Škoda partner or visit www.skoda-auto.de/connect.
