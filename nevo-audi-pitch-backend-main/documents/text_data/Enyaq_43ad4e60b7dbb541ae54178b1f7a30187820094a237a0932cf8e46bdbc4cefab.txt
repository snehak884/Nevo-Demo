Škoda Enyaq Coupé – Connectivity  Škoda Connect  Through Škoda Connect¹, you have access to two types of online services. While Infotainment Online offers you real-time information services, Care Connect focuses on service and safety features. With Škoda Connect, you can remotely control selected vehicle functions via the MyŠkoda App and benefit from practical assistance services.  1. Infotainment Online and Care Connect are available through Škoda Connect. Services are model-dependent, partly chargeable, and their availability depends on the country and mobile reception. Registration with Škoda Auto a.s. is required, except for e Call. Use of Infotainment Online requires a Škoda navigation system. Depending on the vehicle's equipment, an additional internet connection and data plan may be required, which may incur additional costs (Wi-Fi hotspot of a mobile device, mobile phone with r SAP or SIM card). Škoda Connect (Remote Access) is controlled via the MyŠkoda App, which requires a smartphone with internet access. For more information about Škoda Connect, please contact your Škoda partner and visit www.skoda-auto.de/connect.
