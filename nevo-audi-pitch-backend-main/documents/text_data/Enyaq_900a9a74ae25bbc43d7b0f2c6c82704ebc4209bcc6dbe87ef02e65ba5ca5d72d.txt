Škoda Enyaq Coupé Interior   Let your imagination run wild   Typical strengths of Škoda vehicles are the above-average space and numerous practical Simply Clever features that you can find and expect everywhere. You can certainly expect both from the Enyaq Coupé. It is the perfect vehicle for every day. Take the trunk, for example: with a capacity of 570 liters in the basic seating arrangement, it offers space for everything you need for your journey. Fold down the rear seats, and you have a total of 1,610 liters available. Added to this are details such as the variable trunk floor - with space for charging cables - and the net system with hooks, which make the trunk even more practical. The standard equipment and the range of accessories are so extensive that you can let your imagination run wild.
