Škoda Superb – Safety Assistants Multi-Collision Brake If the vehicle is involved in an accident and the airbags are deployed, there is a possibility that it may continue to roll uncontrollably. To prevent secondary collisions, the Multi-Collision Brake slows down the vehicle and brings it to a stop. The Multi-Collision Brake is part of the electronic stability control (ESC): a safety feature that activates the braking system after a collision to avoid further damage.
