Škoda Superb – Safety Takes Priority   Emergency Call Function "e Call+" The Superb is equipped with the automatic emergency call function "e Call+", which is available to you free of charge for the entire lifetime of the vehicle. As soon as the airbag control unit detects a serious accident, the vehicle automatically sends an emergency call to the emergency center. Data such as location, direction, number of vehicle occupants, and the severity of the accident are transmitted, enabling appropriate emergency services to respond as quickly as possible. The system can also be manually activated at any time using buttons in the roof module.
