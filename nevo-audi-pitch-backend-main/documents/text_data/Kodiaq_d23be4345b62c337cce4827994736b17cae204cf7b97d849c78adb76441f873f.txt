Škoda Kodiaq – Assistance Systems Emergency Assistant "Emergency Assist" The Emergency Assistant ("Emergency Assist") intervenes when the driver becomes incapacitated. If the system detects no steering movement or any other action from the driver within a specified period, a warning signal sounds. If the driver does not respond, the assistant triggers a slight turn. If there is still no response from the driver, the vehicle is automatically brought to a stop and the warning lights are activated.
