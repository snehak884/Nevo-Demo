Škoda Superb – Connectivity Smart Link Škoda's Wireless Smart Link technology^1 enables the wireless connection of smartphones with the vehicle's infotainment system. This allows certain functions of the mobile device, such as navigation, music, or calls, to be directly controlled via the infotainment system without the need to physically connect the smartphone via a USB cable. Wireless Smart Link works with all common smartphones and both operating systems, Android (Android Auto) and i OS (Apple Car Play). Additionally, thanks to Smart Link+ in the MyŠkoda app, you can access interesting live data from your drive at any time, such as fuel consumption, driving dynamics, or service information. ^1. Information on terms of use and compatibility of Smart Link can be found at skoda-auto.de/smartlink.
