Škoda Octavia Combi – Travel Assist Adaptive Distance Assistant "Adaptive Cruise Control" The Predictive Distance Assistant ("Predictive/Adaptive Cruise Control", p ACC) extends the functions of the adaptive distance assistant. It monitors the traffic ahead, processes recognized traffic signs, and speed limits stored in the navigation system, and adjusts the speed in a timely manner, such as when approaching a curve, a roundabout, or an intersection, or when reacting to speed limits.
