Octavia Combi – Function meets Design All important information always in view The head-up display projects the current speed, navigation instructions, and other information directly onto the windshield. The 10" Digital Cockpit offers various layouts with detailed information and can be configured to your personal preferences. The infotainment display, up to 13" in size, features Apple Car Play and Android Auto and provides the latest information from Škoda Connect online services¹.  1. Infotainment Online and Care Connect are available under Škoda Connect. The services are model-dependent, partially chargeable, and their availability depends on the country and mobile network reception. Registration with Škoda Auto a.s. is required, except for e Call. The use of Infotainment Online is only possible with a Škoda navigation system. Care Connect (Remote Access) is controlled via the MyŠkoda App, which requires a smartphone (i OS or Android) with internet access. For more information about Škoda Connect, contact your Škoda partner or visit www.skoda-auto.de/connect.
