Škoda Enyaq Coupé: Travel Assist Travel Assist System The Travel Assist combines the functions of several assistance systems, which we introduce on the following pages. Connected together, they make driving easier and provide greater travel comfort. Once activated, the Travel Assist supports you by being able to stop and start again, keep the vehicle in its lane, follow other vehicles in a traffic jam, or display the surroundings on a screen in front of you.
