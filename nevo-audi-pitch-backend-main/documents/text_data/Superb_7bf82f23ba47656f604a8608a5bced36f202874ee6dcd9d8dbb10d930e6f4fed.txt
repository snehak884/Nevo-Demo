Škoda Superb – Assistance Systems Emergency Assistant "Emergency Assist" The Emergency Assistant ("Emergency Assist") intervenes when the driver becomes incapacitated. If the system does not detect any steering movement or other action from the driver within a specified period, a warning signal sounds. If the driver does not respond, the assistant triggers a slight swerve. If there is still no response from the driver, the vehicle is automatically brought to a stop and the hazard lights are activated.
