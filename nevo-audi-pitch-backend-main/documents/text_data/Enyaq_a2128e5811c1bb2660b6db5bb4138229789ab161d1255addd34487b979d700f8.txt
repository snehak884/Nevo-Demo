Enyaq Coupé: Design Freedom to the sky The Enyaq Coupé comes standard with a panoramic glass roof. The front and rear windows form a unified design and emphasize the attractiveness of the vehicle. The panoramic roof is permanently installed, and the front and rear windows are separated from each other. From the inside, the panoramic roof occupies the majority of the ceiling area. The roof is made of laminated glass with light permeability.
