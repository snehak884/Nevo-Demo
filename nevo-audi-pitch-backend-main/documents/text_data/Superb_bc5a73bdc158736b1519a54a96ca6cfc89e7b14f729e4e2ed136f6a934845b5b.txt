Infotainment Online and Care Connect are available under Škoda Connect. The services are model-dependent, partly chargeable, and their availability depends on the country and mobile reception. Registration with Škoda Auto a.s. is required, except for e Call. Use of Infotainment Online only with Škoda navigation systems. Depending on the vehicle's equipment, an additional internet connection and data usage may be required, which might incur additional costs (Wi-Fi hotspots via a mobile device). A mobile phone with r SAP or a SIM card with a data plan and Remote Access is controlled with the MyŠkoda App, which requires a smartphone (i OS or Android) with internet access.
