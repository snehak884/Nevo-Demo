Škoda Octavia Combi – Connectivity 5 USB-C ports and phone box with inductive charging function The Octavia Combi is equipped with four USB-C fast charging ports (45 W) – two in the front and two in the rear. A fifth USB-C port (15 W) is located in the rear-view mirror for charging a camera. In the phone box, smartphones can be charged inductively with up to 15 W of power and active cooling.
