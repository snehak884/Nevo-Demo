Škoda Superb - LED headlights LED taillights Škoda also installs LED technology in the taillights as standard equipment. The optional top version of the taillights features a welcome effect and dynamic indicators. These are divided into three segments and shine with a refined design as well as illuminated crystal elements. The new wide C-light signature extends to the center of the tailgate. Below it, a red light strip with a crystalline structure connects the two taillights.
