Škoda Enyaq Coupé: Interior Everything in control, always accessible. You can adjust all the basic functions of the vehicle via the 13" infotainment screen, such as the air conditioning and safety assistants. Additionally, the Enyaq Coupé offers a variety of connectivity options, including USB-C ports, the possibility of a wireless connection with your smartphone, and remote control via the Škoda Connect services¹. The interior also includes the Virtual Cockpit with a 5.3" display, which shows only the most important information. It can be complemented by an Augmented Reality (AR) Head-up Display, which is particularly praised by tech enthusiasts but is beneficial for everyone as it makes driving not only more comfortable but also safer. ¹. Under Škoda Connect, Infotainment Online and Care Connect are available. The services are model-dependent, partly chargeable, and their availability depends on the country and mobile network reception. For Infotainment Online, voice and data connections are required. Registration with Škoda Auto a.s. is required, except for e Call. Use of the Infotainment Online services only with Škoda navigation system. Depending on the vehicle's equipment, additional internet access and data tariffs may be required, which may incur additional costs (WLAN hotspot of a mobile device, mobile phone with r SAP, or SIM card with data service). Care Connect (Remote Access) is controlled via the MyŠkoda App, which requires a smartphone with internet access. For more information about Škoda Connect, contact your Škoda partner or visit www.skoda-auto.de/connect.
