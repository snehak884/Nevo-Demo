Škoda Kodiaq – Technology   Adaptive Chassis Control Plus (DCC Plus)   The Kodiaq is optionally available with the new generation of Dynamic Chassis Control (DCC Plus) with driving mode selection. DCC Plus allows you to adjust the vehicle's driving behavior to your personal preferences: Normal, Sport, or Comfort – a chassis setup according to your wishes. The new generation offers faster control of the shock absorbers and a wider range of damping characteristics, resulting in improved driving dynamics and higher comfort.
