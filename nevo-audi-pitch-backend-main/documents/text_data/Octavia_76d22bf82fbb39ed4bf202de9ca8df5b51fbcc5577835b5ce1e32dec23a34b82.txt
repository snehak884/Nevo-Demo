Škoda Octavia Combi – Your Number Safe Driver Assistance Systems The Octavia Combi is equipped with state-of-the-art assistance systems that protect both you and your passengers, as well as all other road users. For example, they monitor oncoming traffic when turning left and bring the vehicle to a standstill in dangerous situations. When you have parked the vehicle, the area behind you is monitored, and you are warned when opening the door if there is a risk of collision with another vehicle or cyclist.
