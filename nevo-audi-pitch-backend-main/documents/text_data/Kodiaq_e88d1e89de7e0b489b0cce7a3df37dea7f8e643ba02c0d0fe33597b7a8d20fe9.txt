Škoda Kodiaq – Connectivity Škoda Connect With Škoda Connect, you have access to two types of online services. While Infotainment Online provides information services in real-time, Care Connect focuses on service and safety features. With Škoda Connect, you can remotely control selected vehicle functions via the MyŠkoda App and benefit from practical assistance services.  1. Infotainment Online and Care Connect are available under Škoda Connect. The services are model-dependent, partially subject to charge, and their availability depends on the country and mobile reception. Registration with Škoda Auto a.s. is required, except for e Call. Use of Infotainment Online is only possible with the Škoda navigation system. Care Connect (Remote Access) is controlled via the MyŠkoda App, for which a smartphone (i OS or Android) with internet access is required. For more information on Škoda Connect, contact your Škoda partner and visit skoda-auto.de/connect.
