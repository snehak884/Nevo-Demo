Škoda Enyaq Coupé – Care Connect i V remote access The Enyaq Coupé also features Škoda Connect services, specifically offered for Škoda electric vehicles.  With Remote Charging, you can check your vehicle's charging status or conveniently start the charging process from your living room. The climate control helps you heat or cool the vehicle's interior before you get in. With the departure planner, the vehicle automatically "wakes up" in time, starts the pre-climatization process so that the interior has the desired temperature at departure.
