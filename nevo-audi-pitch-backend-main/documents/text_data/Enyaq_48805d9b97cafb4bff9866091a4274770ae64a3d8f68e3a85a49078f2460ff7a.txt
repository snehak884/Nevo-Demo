Škoda Enyaq Coupé: Interior New concept, same quality The new interior design offers you the familiar quality, with even better space and many clever ideas. The dashboard is shaped to resemble the hood with its projection for the Škoda logo. It is equipped with a similar elevation that supports the hand when operating the infotainment screen. Naturally, as you are accustomed to from Škoda, there are numerous storage compartments for all passengers.
