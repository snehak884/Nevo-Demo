Škoda Kodiaq - Easy Parking Surround View Camera "Area View" The optional driver assistance system "Area View" with surround view cameras in the front and rear areas as well as in the exterior mirrors offers you optimal support in all traffic situations in the Škoda Kodiaq. Thanks to the cameras, you receive various views of your immediate vehicle surroundings in real-time on your onboard display. Modern wide-angle lenses are used, providing you with a bird's eye view as well as 180-degree images of the areas in front of and behind the vehicle.
