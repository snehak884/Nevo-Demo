Drive and Steering All-Wheel Drive The Å koda Superb is equipped with the latest generation of electronically controlled multi-plate clutch. The state-of-the-art all-wheel drive ensures optimal driving dynamics, a high level of active safety, and low fuel consumption. The distribution of driving power to all four wheels is automatic and depends on driving conditions. The ideal torque for the rear axle is calculated by the control unit. Adjustments are possible through variable pressure on the clutch plates.
