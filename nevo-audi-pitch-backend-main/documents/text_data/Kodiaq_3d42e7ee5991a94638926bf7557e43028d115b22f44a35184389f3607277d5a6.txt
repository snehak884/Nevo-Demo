Škoda Kodiaq – Safety Assistants Multi-Collision Brake If the vehicle is involved in an accident and the airbags are activated, there is a possibility that it could continue to roll uncontrollably. To prevent subsequent collisions, the Multi-Collision Brake slows down the vehicle and brings it to a stop. The Multi-Collision Brake is part of the Electronic Stability Control (ESC): a safety feature that activates the braking system after a collision to prevent further damage.
