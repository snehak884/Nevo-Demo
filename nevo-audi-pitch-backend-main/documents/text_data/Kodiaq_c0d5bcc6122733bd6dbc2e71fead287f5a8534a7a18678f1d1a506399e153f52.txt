Driving and Steering All-Wheel Drive The Å koda Kodiaq is available with an electronically controlled multi-plate clutch. The modern all-wheel drive ensures optimal driving dynamics, highest active safety, and economical consumption. The distribution of torque to all four wheels occurs automatically and depends on the driving situation. The ideal torque for the rear axle is calculated by the control unit. The adjustment is made by variable pressure on the clutch plates.
