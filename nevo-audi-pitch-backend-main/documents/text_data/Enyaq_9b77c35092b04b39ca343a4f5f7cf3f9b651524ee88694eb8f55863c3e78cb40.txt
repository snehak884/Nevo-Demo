Škoda Enyaq Coupé: LED Lighting Full LED Matrix Headlights The headlights of the Škoda Enyaq Coupé use LEDs for all functions. The many advantages of LED technology include their longer lifespan and lower power consumption. Additionally, they feature side lights and the innovative Matrix function. New is also the all-weather light function, which replaces the conventional fog lights. This light mode improves road illumination in poor visibility conditions.
