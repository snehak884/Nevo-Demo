Škoda Superb – Easy Parking   Parking Assistance and Maneuver Brake Assist "Manoeuvre Assist"   The parking sensors of the parking assistance and brake assistant ("Manoeuvre Assist") provide support when parking and unparking by warning of the danger of colliding with obstacles. The maneuver brake assistant detects obstacles that are in front of and behind the vehicle and can apply the brakes to prevent a collision.
