Škoda Superb – Smart and spacious  Simply beautiful The interior is dominated by the multifunction steering wheel with the new Škoda logo in the center and trim elements in a unique Unique Dark Chrome. The freestanding, modern touchscreen of the infotainment system protrudes from the decoratively ribbed dashboard and is always within your reach. By moving the automatic gear selector to the steering wheel, the center console offers more space and appears tidier. The ambient lighting in the dashboard and door panels contributes to the feel-good atmosphere of the interior.
