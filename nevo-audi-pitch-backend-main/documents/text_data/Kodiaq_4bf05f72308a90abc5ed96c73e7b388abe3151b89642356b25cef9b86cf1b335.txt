Škoda Kodiaq – Connectivity   SOS emergency call function   Certain Škoda Connect services1 are activated via the buttons on the roof module. e Call, the red SOS button on the right, immediately requests assistance if you are involved in a serious traffic accident or witness one. In case of a breakdown, you can contact your service center using the middle button. If you need information, the left button connects you to the Škoda Infoline.   1. Škoda Connect includes Infotainment Online and Care Connect. The services depend on the model, may be partially subject to charges, and their availability depends on the country and mobile reception. Registration with Škoda Auto a.s. is required, except for e Call. Use of Infotainment Online requires a Škoda navigation system. Depending on the equipment of the vehicle, an additional internet access and data tariff may be required, which may incur additional costs (e.g., mobile device Wi-Fi hotspot, mobile phone with SIMAP or SIM card with data package). Škoda Connect (Remote Access) is controlled with the MyŠkoda Essentials App, for which a smartphone (i OS or Android) with internet access is required. Further information on Škoda Connect is available from your Škoda partner and at www.skoda-auto.de/connect.
