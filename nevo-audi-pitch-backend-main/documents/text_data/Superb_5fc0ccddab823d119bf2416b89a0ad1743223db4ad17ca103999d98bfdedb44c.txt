Škoda Superb – Easy Parking Trained Parking "Trained Park Assist" The Trained Parking function ("Trained Park Assist") offers an automatic parking maneuver into a parking space located in a familiar environment, such as at home. The vehicle automatically controls the steering, brakes, and driving direction (forward or backward) while constantly monitoring the surroundings, detecting obstacles and pedestrians, and preventing collisions with them. Thus, with the help of the assistant, the vehicle can park in a familiar environment over a longer distance (up to 50 m) into a parking space.
