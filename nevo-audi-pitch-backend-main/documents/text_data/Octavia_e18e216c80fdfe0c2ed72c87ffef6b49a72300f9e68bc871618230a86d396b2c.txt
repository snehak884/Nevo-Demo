Škoda Octavia Combi – Travel Assist Emergency Assist "Emergency Assist" The Emergency Assist intervenes in case of driver incapacitation. If the system does not detect any steering movement or other action within a specified period, a warning signal sounds. If there is no response, the Emergency Assist is activated, which brings the vehicle to a stop and activates the hazard warning lights. An emergency call is automatically made, and the horn and interior lighting are activated.
