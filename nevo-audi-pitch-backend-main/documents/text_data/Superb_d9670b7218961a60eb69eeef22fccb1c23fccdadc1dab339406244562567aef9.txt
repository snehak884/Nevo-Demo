Škoda Superb – Connectivity SOS Emergency Call Function Certain Škoda Connect services¹ are activated using the buttons on the roof module. e Call, the red SOS button on the right, immediately requests help if you are involved in a serious traffic accident or witness one. In case of a breakdown, you can contact your service center with the middle button. If you want information, the left button connects you to the Škoda Infoline.  ¹ Škoda Connect includes Infotainment Online and Care Connect. Services depend on the model, are sometimes chargeable, and their availability depends on the country and mobile network reception. Registration with Škoda Auto a. S. is required, except for e Call. Use of Infotainment Online is only possible with a Škoda navigation system. Depending on the vehicle's equipment, an additional internet connection and data plan may be required, which may incur additional costs (mobile device's WLAN hotspot, mobile phone with r SAP, or SIM card with data access). Škoda Connect (Remote Access) is controlled using the MyŠkoda Essentials App, for which a smartphone or device with internet access is required. For more information on Škoda Connect, please contact your Škoda partner or visit skoda-auto.de/connect.
