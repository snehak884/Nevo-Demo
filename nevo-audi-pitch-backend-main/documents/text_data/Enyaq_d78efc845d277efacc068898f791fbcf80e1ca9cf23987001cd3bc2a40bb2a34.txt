Škoda Enyaq Coupé: E-Mobility An SUV with rear-wheel or all-wheel drive Škoda brings back rear-wheel drive with all its advantages. When you sit behind the wheel, the first thing you'll notice is the improved traction during acceleration. Other advantages include a smaller turning circle and a smoother driving experience. The electric motor mounted on the rear axle can be complemented by another electric motor on the front axle. This transforms the Enyaq Coupé into an all-wheel-drive vehicle.
