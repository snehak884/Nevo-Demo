Dorotheenstrasse   Latest Infotainment   The Škoda Enyaq Coupé is equipped with an infotainment system featuring a 13-inch display, which includes a touch slider. This allows for volume control of the music, zooming in and out of the navigation map, or adjusting the air conditioning temperature. Smart Link enables the use and control of smartphone apps via the display of the infotainment system. Bluetooth and a Wi-Fi hotspot connect the vehicle with the phone. The infotainment system also comes standard with DAB, 2 x USB-C, and eight speakers. Car Play
