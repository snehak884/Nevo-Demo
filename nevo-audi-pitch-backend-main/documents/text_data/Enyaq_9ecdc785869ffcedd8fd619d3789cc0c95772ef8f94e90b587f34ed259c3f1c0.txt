Škoda Enyaq Coupé: Technology Safety is a priority The Enyaq Coupé is equipped with a variety of active and passive safety systems that ensure the safety of occupants and other road users. Active safety includes elements and systems designed to prevent hazardous situations or impending accidents. Passive safety encompasses design features and characteristics of the vehicle that aim to mitigate the impact of an accident for the occupants and all other parties involved.
