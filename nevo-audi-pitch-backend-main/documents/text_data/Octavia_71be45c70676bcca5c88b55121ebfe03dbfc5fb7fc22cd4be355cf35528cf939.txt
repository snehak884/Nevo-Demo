Octavia Combi – Innovative Technology Hybrid technology for lower consumption The Škoda Octavia Combi is also available with mild-hybrid technology, which improves fuel efficiency and reduces CO2 emissions. The gasoline engine is supported by a powerful battery, an electric motor, and a part of the electrical network that is powered by 48 volts. This allows the vehicle to completely turn off the combustion engine when, for example, it is traveling at low speed. 1.
