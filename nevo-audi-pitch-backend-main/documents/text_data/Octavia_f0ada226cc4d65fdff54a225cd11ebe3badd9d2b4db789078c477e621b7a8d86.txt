Å koda Octavia Combi safety assists Attention and fatigue detection "Driver Alert" The system detects signs of fatigue and reduces the risk of a fatigue-related dangerous situation. It continuously monitors and evaluates driving and steering behavior, and detects potential deviations at speeds over 65 km/h. If the system detects driver fatigue, a warning tone sounds and a message in the virtual cockpit informs the driver that they should take a break.
